Majestic woman in flowing robes, embodying beauty and sorrow

Found in Japanese mythology, notably in the Kojiki and Nihon Shoki

Ethnicity:
Japanese Shinto deity

Dynasty:

Creation of Japan:

In the opening myth of Japan, Izanami and Izanagi stirred the oceans with the jeweled spear of heaven, creating the Japanese islands. They then married and gave birth to numerous nature deities.
